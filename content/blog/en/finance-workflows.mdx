Finance teams spend 40–60% of their time on repetitive tasks that can be automated. It's not about needing more hands — it's about excess manual processes. Here's what to automate first.

## Top 10 processes to automate

### 1. Bank reconciliation
**Before**: review movements line by line vs accounting.
**After**: automatic matching with rules, only exceptions to human.
**Stack**: Xero/QuickBooks + rules, or n8n + bank APIs.

### 2. Vendor invoice processing
**Before**: type PDF data into ERP.
**After**: OCR + AI extracts fields, human validation, automatic load.
**Stack**: Rossum, Hypatos, Klippa.

### 3. Collections
**Before**: review AR and email one by one.
**After**: workflow sends escalated reminders (D-3, D+0, D+7, D+14) with personalization.
**Stack**: Chaser, Upflow, or custom n8n.

### 4. Executive reports
**Before**: copy from systems to Excel on Mondays.
**After**: dashboard self-refreshes, alerts on deviation.
**Stack**: Metabase, Power BI, Looker connected to sources.

### 5. Expense management
**Before**: employee tapes receipts, accountant enters them.
**After**: mobile app, photo, OCR, digital approval, auto-reimbursement.
**Stack**: Brex, Ramp, Pleo, SAP Concur.

### 6. Month-end close
**Before**: 5 days of manual adjustments.
**After**: scripts validate integrity, auto-generate entries, alert differences.
**Stack**: FloQast, BlackLine, or custom scripts.

### 7. Vendor onboarding
**Before**: PDF form emails, manual follow-ups.
**After**: self-service portal, auto-validation of docs, ERP integration.
**Stack**: Tipalti, Coupa.

### 8. Vendor payments
**Before**: manually load each into the bank.
**After**: automatic batch with digital approval, post-reconciliation automated.

### 9. Cash flow forecast
**Before**: Excel manually updated each Monday.
**After**: model pulling bank + ERP + CRM data, auto-refreshes.
**Stack**: Causal, Cube, or custom models.

### 10. Compliance and regulatory reports
**Before**: manual data extraction for each report.
**After**: pipeline auto-generating and verifying formats.

## How to prioritize

Per candidate process, calculate:
- **Volume**: how many times/month?
- **Time**: how long each time?
- **Human error rate**: high → automation also improves quality.
- **Implementation cost**: vs SaaS vs custom.

Start with the best hours-saved / cost ratio.

## Typical ROI

6-person finance team, 5 automated processes:

| Process | Hours saved/month |
|---|---|
| Vendor invoices | 60 |
| Collections | 40 |
| Reports | 30 |
| Bank reconciliation | 50 |
| Month-end close | 80 |
| **Total** | **260 hrs/mo** |

Equivalent to ~1.5 FTE freed for higher-value tasks (analysis, control, improvement).

## Common mistakes

- **Automating broken processes**: bad process automated amplifies the problem.
- **No exception handling**: when rule doesn't apply, what?
- **No auditability**: a transaction must be traceable step by step.
- **No data governance**: bad input = worse output.
