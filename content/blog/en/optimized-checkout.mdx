Average e-commerce cart abandonment exceeds **70%**. Every point you recover is direct revenue. Done right, checkout optimization can move conversion 25–40% without changing product or traffic.

## The 7 friction points that kill sales

1. **Forced registration before purchase**. Solution: always offer guest checkout.
2. **Too many fields**. Ask only what's needed to ship and charge.
3. **Surprise costs at the end**: shipping, taxes. Show upfront.
4. **Confusing steps**: clear linear progress bar.
5. **Few payment methods**: include Apple Pay, Google Pay, wallet/link.
6. **Cryptic validation errors**: instant per-field feedback.
7. **Slowness**: every extra second of load = -7% conversion.

## Proven patterns

### Single-page checkout
For digital products and small carts. Reduces abandonment 15–25%.

### Express checkout above the fold
Apple Pay, Google Pay above all. Closes sales in 2 taps.

### Smart autofill
Card type by BIN, ZIP by country, city by ZIP. Each autofill = less friction.

### Visible progression
"Step 2 of 3" gives confidence. Uncertainty drives abandonment.

### Cart recovery
Email + WhatsApp at t+1h, t+24h, t+72h. Recovers 8–15% of abandoned cart.

## The metric that matters

Don't look at "global conversion rate". Look at **funnel-stage conversion**:

- Cart → Checkout start
- Start → Data complete
- Data → Payment attempt
- Payment → Order completed

The bottleneck tells you where to optimize.

## Real case

B2C client at 1.8% conversion. Changes:
- Guest checkout
- Express Apple/Google Pay above
- Reduced 11 to 6 fields
- Costs visible from cart

**Result in 60 days**: +34% conversion, +28% revenue (same traffic).
