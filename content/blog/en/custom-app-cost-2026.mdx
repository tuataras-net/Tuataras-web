How much does a custom app cost? The honest answer: it depends. But we can give you realistic ranges and help you understand the drivers so you don't get caught mid-project.

## Typical 2026 ranges

| Project type | USD range | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Functional MVP (web or mobile) | $25k – $60k | 8–14 wks |
| Initial B2B SaaS product | $60k – $180k | 3–6 mo |
| Custom e-commerce platform | $80k – $250k | 4–8 mo |
| Complex enterprise system | $200k – $1M+ | 6–18 mo |

## What drives the price up

- **Integrations**: each external connection adds 1–4 weeks.
- **Compliance**: GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS can double QA and architecture cost.
- **Bespoke UX** vs leveraging an existing design system.
- **Day-1 hyperscale architecture**: building for 1M users from MVP is over-engineering.

## What drives it down (without losing quality)

- **Real MVP first**: don't build v3 before validating v1.
- **Modern productive stack**: Next.js + Supabase/Postgres beats on-prem builds.
- **Off-the-shelf components**: design systems, UI libs, managed auth.
- **Small senior team** vs large junior team.

## Engagement models

- **Fixed price**: good for clear, rigid scope. Risk: scope creep.
- **Time & materials**: flexibility but requires trust and transparent reporting.
- **Sprint-based / dedicated team**: most common in 2026 for evolving product.

## Mistakes that blow the budget

1. No success metrics defined upfront.
2. Architecture decisions made without discovery.
3. Switching vendor mid-project.
4. "While you're at it, also add..."
