A well-built chatbot closes sales and resolves queries 24/7. Poorly built, it frustrates and pushes customers away. The difference isn't the AI model — it's experience design.

## When YES it makes sense

- **High-volume repetitive queries** (FAQ, L1 support, product info).
- **24/7 operation** you can't cover with humans.
- **Lead qualification** on B2B sites.
- **Onboarding** for complex products.
- **E-commerce** with broad catalog (search assistant).

## When NO

- Volume doesn't justify investment (&lt;200 queries/mo).
- Business depends on close human relationship (premium consulting, high ticket).
- No base content to train on (FAQs, manuals, cases).
- No capacity to iterate in first 90 days.

## Modern 2026 stack

### No-code platforms
- **Intercom Fin / Zendesk AI**: integrated with existing CRM.
- **Tidio / Crisp**: SMBs, setup in hours.
- **Voiceflow**: complex visual flows.

### Custom with LLMs
- **Claude / GPT-5 / Gemini** + **RAG** over knowledge base.
- **LangChain / LlamaIndex** for orchestration.
- **Pinecone / Weaviate / pgvector** for embeddings.

### For product
- **Vapi / Bland**: voice chatbots.
- **Botpress**: open source, full control.

## Design that works

### 1. Honesty from start
"Hi, I'm an AI assistant. If you need a human, just say so and I'll connect you." Nothing destroys faster than pretending to be human.

### 2. Ability to escalate to human
When bot can't or customer asks, instant transfer with context. Without this, chatbot is trap, not service.

### 3. RAG over verifiable sources
Bot only responds with info from your docs. If it doesn't know, it says so. No invention (hallucinations).

### 4. Conversation memory
Don't ask same thing 3 times. Use prior context. Consistent tone.

### 5. Actions, not just answers
2026 bots do things: schedule meetings, create tickets, process returns, subscribe.

## Metrics that matter

| Metric | Good | Excellent |
|---|---|---|
| Resolution without human | 40% | 70%+ |
| CSAT | >4/5 | >4.5/5 |
| Avg response time | &lt;5s | &lt;2s |
| Escalation when needed | 100% | 100% |
| Lead/sale conversion | Baseline | +25% vs no bot |

## Fatal mistakes

- **Lead-grabbing bot** without real user value.
- **No human fallback** during business hours.
- **No knowledge base updates**: goes stale fast.
- **No tests**: nobody checks edge cases.
- **Forcing chat** when user just wanted to read.

## Real case

Fashion e-commerce with 8k queries/month. Pre-bot: avg response time 4h, conversion rate 2.1%. Post-bot with AI + human handoff: 70% queries solved in &lt;1min, conversion rate 2.9% (+38%), CSAT 4.6.
