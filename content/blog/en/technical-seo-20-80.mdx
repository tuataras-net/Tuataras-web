80% of SEO effort goes into things that move little. The 20% that does move are invisible technical fundamentals — invisible to users, fundamental to Google. Here's what they are.

## The 7 pillars of technical SEO

### 1. Core Web Vitals
**LCP &lt;2.5s, INP &lt;200ms, CLS &lt;0.1.** Google explicitly penalizes failing sites. And **easiest to improve** with quick wins (images, JS).

### 2. Mobile-first indexing
Google indexes the mobile version. If your mobile is responsive-compressed instead of mobile-first, you lose.

### 3. Correct semantic HTML
- 1 `<h1>` per page.
- Heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3 without skipping).
- Semantic tags (`<article>`, `<nav>`, `<main>`).
- Alt on all informative images.

### 4. Schema markup
Structured data helping Google understand what your page is: product, article, FAQ, breadcrumb, organization. Enables rich snippets that boost CTR.

### 5. Clean stable URLs
- Short, descriptive, with keywords.
- No unnecessary params.
- 301 when changing — never 302 or no redirect.

### 6. Internal linking
Internal link architecture distributes authority. Important pages should receive more relevant internal links.

### 7. Crawl budget
Google assigns limited resources to your site. Don't waste with:
- Duplicate URLs (bad canonical).
- Thin valueless pages.
- Infinite loops from bad pagination.

## The 10 highest-impact actions

1. **Screaming Frog audit** and fix all 4xx/5xx.
2. **Lighthouse 90+** on home and top 10 pages.
3. **Sitemap.xml** updated and submitted to Search Console.
4. **Robots.txt** correct, not blocking what should index.
5. **Schema** on all type pages (articles, products, FAQs).
6. **WebP/AVIF images** with explicit dimensions.
7. **HTTPS** and http → https redirects without loops.
8. **Correct hreflang** if multi-language.
9. **Page speed** within Core Web Vitals.
10. **Internal linking** reviewed, no orphans on important.

## Tools you actually need

- **Google Search Console** (free, fundamental).
- **Screaming Frog** (free up to 500 URLs, $250/yr if more).
- **Ahrefs / SEMrush / Sistrix** (one, not all three).
- **PageSpeed Insights / WebPageTest** (free).
- **Schema markup validator** (free, Google).

## Ranking-killer mistakes

- **Changing URLs without 301 redirects**: instant drop.
- **Blocking via robots.txt** pages you want indexed.
- **Duplicate content** without canonical.
- **JavaScript hiding content from crawler** (bad client-side rendering).
- **Orphan sites**: pages without internal links to them.

## What NO LONGER works in 2026

- **Keyword stuffing**: Google detects and penalizes.
- **Unedited AI-generated content**: low-quality penalty.
- **Mass purchased backlinks**: detected by algorithm.
- **Hidden text** or any black-hat technique.

## What WORKS in 2026

- **E-E-A-T**: Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness. Showing who writes and why you trust.
- **Useful content first**: algorithm rewards pages effectively solving user intent.
- **Speed and CWV**: mentioned, weight keeps growing.
- **AI Overview optimization**: structuring content for Google AI to cite.

## Real case

B2B site stuck at 3k organic/mo. Technical audit revealed: 240 404 errors, CWV in red, schema missing, outdated sitemap.

Post-fix (8 weeks): organic 3k → 8.4k/mo (+180%) without touching content. Avg position improved 12 places.
