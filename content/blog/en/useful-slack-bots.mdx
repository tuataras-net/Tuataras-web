Slack can be productivity or noise. Well-designed bots make the difference: team gets weekly hours back and comms improve. Poorly designed, just notifications everyone mutes.

## Top 10 bots with best ROI

### 1. Async standup
- Bot asks each: "What did you do yesterday? Today? Blockers?"
- Consolidates in single channel.
- Replaces daily 15-min × N people meeting.
**Tools**: Geekbot, Range, custom with Slack API.

### 2. Vacation / OOO
- `/vacation` command requests days.
- Manager approves with emoji.
- Marks calendar and notifies team.

### 3. Feedback / kudos
- `/kudos @person reason` command.
- Visible in culture channel.
- Recognition stays for reviews.

### 4. New hire onboarding
- When someone joins, bot sends gradual messages with key info.
- Introduces buddy.
- Collects feedback at days 30/60/90.

### 5. Ticket / PR reminders
- "Your PR has been waiting 3 days for review".
- "Ticket #234 overdue 2h".
- No spam: only requires action.

### 6. Smart alerts
- Metric X drops 30% → bot alerts right channel with context.
- Only actionable alerts, not random logs.

### 7. Internal AI FAQ
- `/ask travel policy` command.
- AI responds with info from your docs (RAG).
- Reduces repeated questions to HR/IT/Legal.

### 8. Quick polls
- `/poll pizza or sushi?`.
- Real-time results.
- Replaces 15 messages of "I vote X".

### 9. Personal reminders
- "Remind me tomorrow 9am to review client X email".
- For Slack-living tasks, stays in Slack.

### 10. Daily digest
- Day summary in very-active channels.
- For who was out or doesn't want to scroll 200 messages.

## Bots that DON'T work

- **Notification bombing**: notifies everything. People mute the channel.
- **No context**: "alert!" without actionable info.
- **Email replacement**: 15 messages for what was 1 email.
- **No way to configure / silence**: zero respect for user.

## Build vs buy

### Buy if...
- Case is generic (standup, OOO, kudos).
- Mature integrated solution exists.
- Your team doesn't want to maintain.

### Build if...
- Case is specific to your operation.
- You want to integrate with internal systems.
- Your team has bandwidth.

## Stack to build

- **Slack Bolt** (Node, Python, Java): official SDK.
- **Hosting**: Vercel/Cloudflare Workers for simple bots.
- **Slash commands + Block Kit** for interactive UIs.
- **n8n / Make**: no-code bots connected to other apps.

## Common build mistakes

- **No Slack API rate limit handling**.
- **Hardcoded tokens** in code.
- **No test environment**: testing in production.
- **Underestimating maintenance**: each bot is software to maintain.

## Real case

80-person company. Adopted 4 bots:
- Async standup: saved 250 hrs/mo (5 meetings × 12 teams).
- AI FAQ: -40% questions to HR.
- PR reminders: -25% lead time.
- Kudos: +12 internal NPS points.

Total monthly cost: $200. Estimated ROI: 50x.
